Game 1 - Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

Click on a cell to place your mark. The first player to get three in a row wins!

Player 1: X   |   Player 2: O

Rules

  • After draw starting player switches.
  • After win winning player starts new round.
  • Giving up by active player awards point to the other player.
  • 'New round' button below playing field resets field.
  • 'Reset game' button below Game Statistics clears statistics and sets starting player to P1.
  • Click the reset button to start a new game.
